# Entry Requirements Reference

This reference document provides additional detail on entry requirements
for popular travel destinations. This is loaded on-demand by the skill
when detailed information is needed.

## Schengen Area (EU)
- 90 days maximum stay in any 180-day period
- No border controls between member states
- Single visa covers all 27 member countries

## Japan
- 90-day visa-free stay for US citizens
- Must have return or onward ticket
- No minimum passport validity beyond your stay

## Australia
- eTA required ($20 AUD fee)
- Apply online at eta.homeaffairs.gov.au
- Passport must be valid for duration of stay

## India
- e-Visa required for most nationalities
- Apply at indianvisaonline.gov.in
- Multiple categories: Tourist, Business, Medical

## Note
Requirements change frequently. Always verify at travel.state.gov or
the destination country's official immigration website.
