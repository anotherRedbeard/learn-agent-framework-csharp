# Expense Policy FAQ

**Q: Do I need a receipt for a $20 coffee meeting?**
A: No. Receipts are only required for expenses over $25.

**Q: Can I fly business class to a 4-hour meeting?**
A: No. Business class is only approved for flights over 6 hours.

**Q: My expense is $600 — who needs to approve it?**
A: Expenses over $500 require manager approval. Submit to your direct manager.

**Q: I forgot to submit last month's expenses. Can I still claim them?**
A: You have 30 days from the expense date. Late submissions require HR approval.

**Q: What counts as a business meal?**
A: Client entertainment, team meals with business purpose, or working meals. Personal meals while traveling are reimbursable up to the $75/day limit.
